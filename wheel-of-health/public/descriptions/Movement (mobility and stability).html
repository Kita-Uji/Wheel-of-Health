How well your body moves through space doing everyday activities. Joint health, posture, coordination, and pain-free movement. What benefit is there from being able to lift heavy weights or running at a good pace if you cannot get off the ground without using your hands?

Why it matters:
How well you move is directly linked with longevity. Movement quality determines how safely and confidently you use your strength.

Low score means:
Stiffness, recurring aches, tight hips or shoulders, and a body that feels older than it should. If not addressed, the body will continue to seize up.

High score means:
Supple, confident movement. You feel capable, stable, and pain-free in both training and daily life. 

A car with a well maintained suspension drives smoothly, and reduces the risk of other damages that occur through wear and tear.
