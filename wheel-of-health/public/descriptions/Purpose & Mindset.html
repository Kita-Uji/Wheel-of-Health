Purpose and mindset reflect your internal framework: clarity of direction, discipline, identity, and self-respect. This wheel governs why you act, not just what you do.

Why it matters:
No plan survives contact with life without the right mindset. Motivation is fleeting. Identity and purpose are stabilising forces that carry behaviour through chaos, stress, and inconvenience.

Low score means:
You drift between bursts of motivation and periods of burnout. Consistency depends on mood, circumstances, or external pressure. Progress is fragile.

High score means:
Your habits align with who you are becoming. Discipline replaces reliance on motivation. Decisions are guided by identity rather than impulse.

Mindset is your compass. Without it, effort still moves you but often in circles.
